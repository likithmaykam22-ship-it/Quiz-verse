# Quiz-verse
**QuizVerse** is an online quiz game that lets you test how much you know about the world... whether that's history, science, sports, music, films, or just random facts.
The whole point of the game is simple: have fun while actually learning something. It's the kind of game you open when you're bored or want to challenge yourself, and before you know it you're three rounds deep trying to beat your last score.
What makes it genuinely interesting is the pressure. You don't just sit and think... you have 20 seconds on a ticking timer, and the faster you answer, the more bonus points you get. That alone makes every question feel like a mini heart attack in the best way.
On top of that, the streak system keeps you hooked. When you're on a 4 or 5 question streak, you really don't want to break it, so every question feels high stakes even if it's about animals or geography.
The fact that you can choose your category and difficulty means it works for everyone. A 12-year-old can play on Easy with Animals, and a trivia enthusiast can go Hard on History and still struggle.
And honestly? The game just looks great. The dark theme, glowing colors, smooth animations, it doesn't feel like a boring school quiz. It feels like something worth playing.
In short, QuizVerse takes something as simple as a quiz and wraps it in just enough excitement to make you want to keep going.
